The Moka PBJ

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is one of my favorite sandwich.
It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice)
  • 1 jar of jelly (your choice, I use strawberry)
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half. Optional: Toast bread before cutting.
